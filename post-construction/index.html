Our Services

Post-Construction
Cleaning

Detailed cleaning after construction or renovation projects removing all dust, debris, and residue to leave your space clean and ready for occupancy.

Overview

From Construction Zone to Move-In Ready

Construction and renovation projects leave behind far more than just a finished space they leave behind layers of fine dust, paint splatter, adhesive residue, sawdust, and debris that standard cleaning simply can't handle. Our post-construction cleaning team is equipped and trained specifically for this type of thorough clean.

We work systematically through the property, treating each surface, fixture, and floor with the appropriate method and product to leave the space genuinely clean not just visually presentable. From a freshly renovated bathroom to a new-build commercial space, we handle it all.

Custom Quotes Available

Post-construction cleaning is priced based on the size of the space and scope of the project. Contact us for a custom quote.

What's Included

  • Dust removal walls, ceilings, surfaces, vents, and all fixtures throughout
  • Windows & glass cleaning off paint, adhesive, and construction film
  • Hard floors sweeping, mopping, and removing construction residue
  • Carpets vacuuming and spot treating any construction dust or marks
  • Fixtures & hardware wiping down new or existing fixtures, outlets, and switch plates
  • Cabinets & counters interior and exterior wiping of all new installations
  • Debris removal sweeping up remaining debris and disposing of waste
