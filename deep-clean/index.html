Deep
Cleaning

A detailed, top-to-bottom clean designed for first-time clients or homes that need extra attention tackling every surface, corner, and hard-to-reach area.

Overview

The Ultimate Reset for Your Home

Our deep cleaning service goes well beyond a standard clean. It's the ideal starting point for new clients, or for homes that have gone a while without a thorough clean. We dedicate extra time and attention to the areas that are typically overlooked baseboards, inside appliances, window sills, light fixtures, and more.

A deep clean is also a great way to prepare a home before listing it for sale, before a large event, or simply to hit the reset button. Once completed, maintaining your home with our regular residential cleaning service becomes much easier.

Recommended For

First-time clients, homes not recently cleaned, pre/post-event prep, pre-sale preparation, and seasonal resets.

Everything in a Standard Clean, Plus:

  • Baseboards wiped and scrubbed throughout the entire home
  • Inside appliances oven interior, microwave, and refrigerator
  • Cabinet interiors wiped inside kitchen and bathroom cabinets
  • Window sills & tracks cleaned and wiped down
  • Grout scrubbing tile grout in bathrooms and kitchen
  • Light fixtures & ceiling fans detailed dusting and wiping
  • Behind & under furniture cleaning accessible spaces typically missed
  • Door frames & switch plates wiped throughout the home
